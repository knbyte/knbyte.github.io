About Me

Hi there! My name is Kai, and I'm passionate about accessibility and inclusion, both in workplaces
and everyday life. I firmly believe that everyone deserves equal opportunities and access to resources,
regardless of their background, circumstances or abilities.


I started my career working in call centers, initially in job coaching and later in supporting students
with their studies by providing and assisting them in getting their Unique Student Identifier numbers (USIs).
Through these experiences, I developed a deep understanding of the importance of accessibility and the challenges
faced by individuals in accessing education and employment opportunities.


One of my key strengths is my ability to connect with people. I enjoy building relationships and creating
a supportive environment where everyone feels valued and included. Additionally, my creativity allows me to think
outside the box and come up with innovative solutions to problems.


Currently, I am studying at 42 The Programming School to develop new skills.
I enjoy tacking coding problems creatively from new angles.


One day I would love to have the opportunity to leverage the new skills I am developing
and passion for accessibility to create more inclusive technologies and environments.
